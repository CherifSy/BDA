It can be computed either by the numerical simulation or empirical equations ([Kozny-Carman equation](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kozeny%E2%80%93Carman_equation)). ## Source of Structure Datasets * Gravitational Sphere Packing Simulation

Mono-sized Loosely Packed Particles

The packed structure will be simulated by the new Gravitational Sphere Packing Simulation. More information about this simulation can be found in [this link](http://scholar.google.com/citations?user=W8oVUI8AAAAJ&hl=en). ## Details of Structure Datasets * Tortousity * Porosity * 2-point statistics ## Proposed Workflow * Implement simulations based on the gravitational sphere packing approach * If we can come up with the numerical results for permeability such as Lattice-Boltzmann method, we can do two parts of work: (1) correlate structure property to permeability (propose new equation); (2) correlate 2-point statistics to permeability through PCA and regression analyses * Compare two approaches: 2-point statistics-permeability and structure-permeability. ## References * [Wong, R.
